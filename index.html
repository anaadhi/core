In light of the worldwide pandemic, we have introduced Digilect, that allows you to vote without ever stepping out into the lashes of COVID-19.

Learn more

The Problem

The pandemic has transformed the routine of the world. Generic methods now compromise safety and health. In such a dire situation, something state or even nation-wide, like voting, has become a major hurdle against the administrative framework.

The Solution

We have devised a method to vote safely from inside home. Digilect is a device specifically built for voting purposes. Similar to a phone in structure, it transfers data to a drone flying over every district, via bluetooth, and not the internet, to prevent exploitation by hackers.

Your vote is your vote

Digilect comes with a fingerprint scanner, which confirms your true identity. It also takes other information by the user for confirmatory purposes. This data is crosschecked via the database of the overhead drone. We ensure that it is you and only you, polling your vote. Your vote is also anonymous, i.e no one will know whom you voted for, to prevent bribing.

When to vote?

The drone is controlled by government approved pilots. The drone will fly over each district between a time interval pre-specified for each district by the government. Citizens must caste their vote between this time interval. In case of emergencies, an exception will be made, if approved by the government.

Secure

The device comes with a thermal sensor, which scans the surroundings and ensures you are isolated, so that your fingerprint is not forcefully registered by any individuak or third party with malicious interests. Please ensure that there is no one is near you within 3 meters of radius while voting. Your vote is secure with us.

Hack-Proof

In case of any tampering or damage caused to the device, Digilect instantly alerts the drone, which then notifies the pilot, who forwards the information to the nearest police station. A fine of 70 usd is charged in case of any serious damage caused to the device.
